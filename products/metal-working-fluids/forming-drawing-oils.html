Specialized lubricants for metal forming and deep drawing operations. Formulated with EP additives and polar compounds to withstand extreme pressure and temperature conditions while providing excellent surface finish and easy removal in degreasing baths.

Key Features & Benefits

Extreme Pressure Resistance

EP additives and lubricity enhancers provide superior protection under high pressure and temperature conditions typical of forming operations.

Excellent Surface Finish

Provides superior surface quality on formed parts, reducing the need for secondary finishing operations and improving product appearance.

No Smoking

Formulated to resist thermal degradation and prevent smoke formation during high-temperature forming operations, ensuring a clean working environment.

Easy Removal

Can be easily removed in standard degreasing baths, simplifying post-forming cleaning processes and reducing cycle times.

Extended Tool Life

Reduces wear on dies, punches, and forming tools, extending tool life and reducing maintenance costs.

Multi-Metal Compatibility

Suitable for forming steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, and other metals and alloys.

Applications

Deep Drawing

Ideal for deep drawing operations of sheet metal into complex shapes, providing excellent lubrication and anti-galling properties.

Stamping & Blanking

Perfect for automotive stamping, blanking, and fine blanking operations requiring precision and clean edges.

Wire Drawing

Suitable for wire drawing operations, reducing friction and improving surface finish on drawn wire products.

Tube Forming

Excellent for tube forming, bending, and hydroforming operations requiring high lubricity and pressure resistance.

Roll Forming

Provides consistent lubrication for continuous roll forming operations of profiles and structural components.

Cold Heading

Suitable for cold heading and forging operations, protecting tools and ensuring dimensional accuracy.
