Advanced chemical solutions for textile manufacturing

Overview

Textile chemicals, also known as textile auxiliaries, are vital in textile production to enhance fabric quality, performance, and characteristics like dye uptake and color fastness. Our comprehensive range includes pretreatment agents, dyeing auxiliaries, finishing chemicals, and performance enhancers for various textile applications.

Dyes

Our dye portfolio includes both natural and synthetic options such as reactive, direct, acid, and disperse dyes. These dyes are formulated to work with mordants and fixatives to achieve vibrant and long-lasting colors on various fiber types.

We offer solutions under Bezema Colour Solutions, providing consistent color quality and excellent fastness properties for diverse textile applications.

Dyeing Auxiliaries

Dyeing auxiliaries including leveling agents, dispersing agents, and retarding agents facilitate uniform and efficient dye absorption. These products prevent issues like rope marks and color bleeding while improving the hand feel of the substrate.

Our range includes Dalewash, Daledye, and Dalefix products designed to enhance textiles and improve color fastness across various dyeing processes.

Printing Auxiliaries

Printing auxiliaries are essential components of textile auxiliary solutions, with pigment printing products being a key offering. These chemicals ensure precise pattern application, color vibrancy, and durability in printed textiles.

Technical Textiles

Our textile auxiliary solutions extend to technical textiles, providing specialized chemicals for high-performance applications. These solutions address the unique requirements of technical textile manufacturing, including enhanced durability, functionality, and performance characteristics.

Technical textile applications benefit from our comprehensive range of finishing chemicals and performance enhancers designed for demanding industrial and specialized uses.
